Return Window

We accept returns for exchange or refund within 30 calendar days of delivery. After 30 days, we may offer store credit or an exchange at our discretion, but refunds are no longer guaranteed.

To be eligible for a return, an item must be:

  • Unused, uninstalled, and in the same condition it was received in
  • In its original manufacturer packaging
  • Accompanied by the original receipt, invoice, or order confirmation

How to Request a Return (RMA)

All returns require a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number before being shipped back. Items sent without an RMA may be refused or returned to the sender at their expense.

  • Contact us with your order number and reason for return to request an RMA
  • RMA numbers are valid for 30 days from the date issued
  • Ship returns fully insured with tracking — we are not responsible for items lost or damaged in return transit
